# NFT sp-coe collection details

The sp-coe collection comprises 290 NFT tokens that played a vital role in securing resources required for market research and the technical development of the COE prototype. This collection was created to attract strategic support during the project's initial phase.

Reward Distribution and Rights of sp-coe Holders:

* Initial Contribution Reward: In commitment to the supporters of the research phase, 1% of the total COE Token supply has been distributed among the 290 NFTs as an Initial Contribution Reward, acknowledging the critical role of this collection in providing initial liquidity and technical stability to the project.
* Specialized Governance: sp-coe holders possess higher voting rights in the DAO structure and play a central role in the project's strategic direction.
* Priority Access: Members who had previously received ap-coe NFTs via Airdrop were given priority access in the contribution process for this collection.
